About Me

Hello and welcome to my web home! My name is Julia, and I’m an aspiring web developer based in Minneapolis. I console.logged “Hello, World” for the first time on August 14, 2017 and I was instantly hooked. I hope to continue pursuing the magic of coding for the rest of my life. Think of me as Dorothy in the Wizard of Oz. I’m starting down the yellow-brick road of learning to code. The journey will not be easy, there will be twists, turns and unexpected setbacks (objects, algorithms, design patterns, oh my!); but there will also be discoveries, new friendships, and plenty of "aha" moments along the way.

This portfolio represents the various stops on my journey to peek behind the curtain of programming magic. When I get there, there will be no need for me to click my heels as I would already be home.
